About the Project

What is ELS?

Thousands of corporate events, conferences, weddings, and public gatherings generate enormous amounts of surplus food and materials — most of which goes to waste while local food banks and charities struggle with demand.

The Event Leftover Redistribution System (ELS/ELRS) bridges this gap with an automated, technology-driven platform that lets event organizers instantly post surplus items, intelligently matches them to verified charities, and coordinates pickups — all in real time.

Core Features

What the System Does

ELS combines cloud infrastructure, AI analytics, and multi-channel notifications into one seamless workflow.

📋

Post Surplus Items

Event organizers post leftover food and materials with item name, quantity, expiry date, and photos in seconds.

🤖

AI-Powered Matching

Smart algorithm analyzes past donation history and real-time demand to match surplus with the most suitable charity.

🔔

Instant Notifications

Multi-channel alerts via email, SMS, and push notifications keep charities informed of available donations immediately.

📦

Inventory Tracking

Real-time inventory dashboard powered by AWS DynamoDB tracks all surplus items, statuses, and donation history.

🗺️

Pickup Coordination

Charities can accept donations and schedule pickups with location details and time windows through the app.

📊

Admin Reports

Admins generate donation history reports, monitor system performance, and review analytics via the dashboard.

Project Roadmap

Development Phases

The project follows a five-phase lifecycle from research to full deployment.

1

Research & Planning Completed

Studied manual redistribution inefficiencies, identified key pain points, and evaluated cloud/AI technology options.

2

System Design Completed

Designed normalized database schema, UI wireframes, DFDs, ERDs, and multi-channel notification architecture.

3

Prototype Development & Testing In Progress

Building a functional prototype with surplus posting, charity notifications, and inventory tracking. Integration and stress testing underway.

4

Refinement & Optimization Planned

Enhance AI donation matching with ML, add real-time stock validation, demand forecasting, and strengthen security protocols.

5

Final Review & Presentation Planned

Full system demo, stakeholder presentation, performance analysis, and deployment-ready documentation.
